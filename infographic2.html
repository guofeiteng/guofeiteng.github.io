The bar chart

The Bar chart shows the schedule and cost variance in all 26 departments. The Bar upper the X-axis means the department complete the task ahead or cost less than planed. The Bar under the X-axis means the department failure to complete the tast on time or cost more than planed.

The goverment can check this bar chart to find out the situation of different department's work, Then decided to change the plan,reward or punish. On the other hand, the citizens would like the know the performance of different department's as well.

Interactive

Move: Move mouse to the bar chart, the detailed data will display

Click: There are two buttons in the right, click them to compare two group data or just display one group data.

Zoom: Users can drag the bar chart to check the detail information in the rectangle which created by mouse, or just click the zoom in and zoom out button in the top right corner.

Pan: After click the pan button, Users can drag the whole bar chart to check different area.

Double Click: Users can drag the bar chart to check the detail information in the rectangle which created by mouse.
